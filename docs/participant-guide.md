# Participant Guide

Guide for participants taking a conversational survey.

---

## How to Access a Survey

You'll receive a survey link or a survey ID from the person who invited you. There are two ways to start:

1. **Direct link** — Click the survey URL (e.g., `https://example.com/survey/{survey-id}`). You'll land on the survey landing page.
2. **Survey ID** — Go to the survey platform, enter your survey ID on the landing page, and click **Start Survey**.

You can optionally enter your name before starting. This is not required.

---

## The Survey Experience

This is not a traditional survey with fixed questions. Instead, the survey is a **conversation** — an AI interviewer asks you questions one at a time, and each follow-up question is tailored to your previous answers.

### How it works

1. **You receive a question** — displayed in a chat-style interface.
2. **You type your answer** — there's no word limit. Answer as briefly or as thoroughly as you like.
3. **The AI reads your answer** and generates a relevant follow-up question that digs deeper into the topic.
4. **This continues** until the survey goal has been sufficiently explored or the maximum number of questions is reached.

### What you'll see

- A **chat interface** showing your conversation history
- A **progress bar** indicating how far along you are (based on the maximum question count)
- The **current question** highlighted for you to answer

### Exiting early

You can **exit the survey at any time** by clicking the exit button. Your responses up to that point are saved. You do not need to complete all questions.

---

## Tips for Providing Good Responses

1. **Be honest and specific.** The AI adapts to your answers — the more detail you provide, the more relevant the follow-up questions will be.
2. **Give examples when you can.** Instead of "It's fine," try "It works well because I can drop off my kids at school and start working by 9:30."
3. **Don't worry about grammar or formatting.** This is a conversation, not an essay.
4. **Take your time.** There's no timer. Think about your answer before submitting.
5. **Stay on topic.** The AI is focused on a specific research goal — your answers are most valuable when they relate to the topic at hand.

---

## What Happens When the Survey Ends

A session ends in one of three ways:

| Ending | What happened |
|--------|--------------|
| **Goal reached** | The AI determined that your answers have sufficiently covered the research goal. You'll see a completion message. |
| **Max questions reached** | You answered the maximum number of questions configured for this survey. |
| **You exited early** | You chose to leave before the survey finished. Your answers are still saved. |

After the survey ends:
- You'll see a **thank-you / completion screen** confirming the survey is done.
- All your responses are saved and available to the survey administrator.
- You do **not** need to do anything else — your participation is complete.

---

## Privacy

- Your responses are stored linked to a session ID, not your personal identity (unless you provided your name).
- The survey administrator can view all responses.
- The AI does not retain memory between different sessions.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I go back and change an answer?**
No. The survey is a forward-moving conversation. Each answer builds on the previous context.

**How long does it take?**
Typically 3–10 minutes, depending on the number of questions and how detailed your responses are.

**What if I don't understand a question?**
Answer as best you can, or say "I'm not sure about that" — the AI will adapt and ask something different.

**Can I take the same survey twice?**
Yes, each session is independent. Starting the survey again creates a new session.
